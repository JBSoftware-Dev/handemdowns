Help

General questions

How do I post an ad?

Posting an ad is quite simple. Click on Create Ad and start filling in the required information. Phone Number is not required, neither are any images but both improve your capability to get rid of an item. Should you decide to upload some images they will be automatically compressed and resized by your devices browser to save your data usage and our server resources.

Is my postal code really required to create an ad.

We provide ads based on your proximity to other ads by their location, postal code is just a general idea of where the ad is located. The postal code field is required but you may use an adjacent postal code or the postal code of a nearby landmark/mall/plaza/store.
An easy way to find nearby postal codes is from Google Maps. You can find a location, long press (in the app) or right-click (on website) to pin the location and view the address and postal code.

How can I improve interest of my item or service?

Handemdowns is new and there may not be many ads in your area but that doesn't mean you shouldn't get the ball rolling. Be sure to include all the relevant information, including images helps and be sure to properly form your title and description for readability.

Do I need to register to use Handemdowns?

Nope! You can start giving items away and free up that space right now! Although if you register you will have the ability to login and track your created ads, you will be able to hold/activate ads and you will be able to track ads you are interested in with the watchlist. Furthermore you will not need to confirm ads whereas annonymous users must confirm their ads by email.
Item Donars

Who can give away items?

Absolutely anybody! If you are below the age of 16 it is strongly recommended to have a parent or guardian help you with the transaction. You do not need to register to create an ad, you can start using Handemdowns right away.

I want to get ride of some items - what are the steps?

The steps involved to create an ad are really simple:
  • Register an account and activate it (Optional)
  • Go to the Create Ad page and fill out the form
  • Confirm Ad if you don't have an account (Optional)
  • Wait a short time for the ad to be automatically activated

Am I allowed to charge?

No, please do not put a price in the title or description. Although we cannot stop this, we encourage users to report any ads that violate this rule. We ask that our users try to keep this platform a commuications channel to exchange free goods and services only.
Recipients

I am interested in aquiring an item from an ad - what are the steps?

All you need to do is:
  • Register an account and activate it (Optional)
  • On the item's page fill out the "Contact Poster" section to send a message the item's owner
  • Wait for a reply from donar, you can now have a conversation through email.

What procautions should I take when aquiring an item?

The best thing to do is be fully aware, if going to a donar residence you should do research into where you are going. Since this is a free exchange there will most likely be no problems whatsoever but it's better to be aware or ask to meet at a place you know.

Is there a limit to the amount of items I can take?

There is no limit at all, in fact most people are probably glad to have their unneeded items removed from their possesion. We ask that you use better judgement and try not to take more than what is needed. The applies particularly to clothes and books. Handemdowns greatly appreciates your cooporation in this matter.
