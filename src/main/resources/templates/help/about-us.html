About Us

Handemdowns is a local classifieds web site to exchange free goods or services. The aim is for refined commications channel where users can easily create ads for free stuff. All ads for items of services will always be FREE! No spam, just a simple and safe way to give away your unneed stuff.

Sometimes people don't want to go through selling an item no matter how cheap it is. There will always be a concern for safety. With free tranactions there is little to be worried about. You can feel good about giving something away and helping someone out. Not to mention your new found space! Handemdowns looks to fill this void.

Handemdowns was started in Toronto and serves most major cities in the US and Canada. Founded by two entrepreneurs looking to provide an alternative to other local classified sites which are tailored to paid services and goods.
